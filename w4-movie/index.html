HOME

Experience 30 years of history, from the 1950s to the early 1980s, from the perspective of Forrest Gump, an Alabama man with an IQ of 75. From a small child, Forrest struggles to make friends due to his physical condition, but befriends a young girl named Jenny. Follow Forrest as he plays college football, gets drafted to the Vietnam War, runs across the continental United States, captain's a shrimping boat, and so much more, all the while trying to reunite with his dear friend, Jenny.
